Biography

Max Emilian Verstappen (born 30 September 1997) is a Dutch racing driver who competes in Formula One for Red Bull Racing. He is the son of former Formula One driver Jos Verstappen.

In 2015, he became the youngest driver to compete in Formula 1 at the age of 17. He won his first World Championship in 2021 and has since become one of the most successful drivers in the history of the sport.
